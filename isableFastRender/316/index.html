316. Come, Let Us Worship and Bow Down

Dave Doherty

Key: G

Come, let us worship and bow down,
Let us kneel before the Lord, our God,
our maker,
For He is our God
And we are the people of His pasture
And the sheep of His hand,
Just the sheep of His hand.
