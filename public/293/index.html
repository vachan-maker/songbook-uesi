293. We Bring Sacrifice of Praise

Kirk Dearman | Key: D

English Transliteration

We bring sacrifice of praise
Unto the house of the Lord, (2)

And we offer unto You,
The sacrifices of thanksgiving,
And we offer unto You,
Our sacrifices of joy.
