334. Spirit of the Living God

Daniel Iverson | Key: F

English Transliteration

Spirit of the living God,
Fall afresh on me,
Spirit of the living God,
Fall afresh on me,
Break me, melt me,
mould me and fill me,
Spirit of the living God,
Fall afresh on me.
