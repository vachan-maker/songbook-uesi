369. I just keep trusting my lord

English Transliteration

Key:

I just keep trusting my Lord
As I walk along,
I just keep trusting my Lord,
And He gives me a song,
Though the storm clouds darken the sky,
O’er the heavenly trail,
I just keep trusting the Lord,
He will never fail
